about my anime

In Japan, Anime/Animation means all forms of animated media. Depending on where you are, the style, the color, the production may be different. My first anime experience is monga which is comic book around 2002. As time goes by, the manga become more like a magazine form, then to digital files, online comic books, 2D anime. In the end, we now have 3D animae. Internet plays pretty big role intern of anime’s evolution. As soon as anime step into the cloud, it has been shared millions people. It speed up the anime industry’s to produce each anime. Now we have this unlimited online resource to watch anime anytime you want.

rental bookstore

The earliest way to get into anime was going to rental bookstore to rent the commicbook. They usually provide a membership card that allows people put credit in their card. One inconic feature in the bookstore is the giant movable book shelf. The other significant cherecter of the commicbook is yellowish kind of paper. Bwfore technolodgy become that advance, paper quality is also not that ideal.

photo book

Back of the time, when the heavy desktops were avalible to people, the commic book will be copied into Floppy Disk. In that way, people can bring the floppy disk to their computer to see the scan version of commic book. It directly took over the rental nookstore's business.

digital book

After internest was widely use, digital commic book start appeared on some website. People can pretty much just search the anime that they want to read and they will find some kind of infomation. Of course, copy rights become the issue. At this point, most rental bookstores had closed down their business unless they change their business model. For instant, some bookstores transform into a cafe or afternoon tea house so the customers will cintinue visit but not necessary for the anime.
